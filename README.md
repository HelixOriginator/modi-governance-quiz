
Modi Governance Quiz

An interactive and immersive quiz platform exploring 12 years of transformative governance in India (2014–2026) through engaging quizzes, modern UI design, gamification, and educational storytelling.

The platform highlights major achievements, reforms, welfare schemes, infrastructure projects, digital transformation, diplomacy milestones, defence modernization, startup growth, and national development initiatives.
